Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

This Privacy Policy describes how Taras Depko ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application, Chronory (the "Service").

1. Information Collection

We prioritize your privacy. Chronory is designed to minimize data collection. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information (PII) unless you voluntarily provide it to us (e.g., via email support).

2. Usage Data

The app may collect anonymous usage data to help us improve the performance and stability of the application. This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.

3. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you (e.g., Apple Services for iCloud sync, if applicable). Please refer to the privacy policies of any third-party service providers used by the app.

4. Security

We value your trust in providing us your information, thus we strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at prufgoods@ukr.net.
